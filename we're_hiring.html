This is how we work

Our approach to work is based on our values. We truly believe that culture eats strategy for breakfast and our people drive the company forward.

Power to our people

We claim ownership of our work. We have a shared commitment to accomplish great things. We seek what is best for SadaPay and take responsibility when we fall short.

Embracing diversity

We believe that diversity fosters true innovation. From experienced bankers to fresh graduates, the team works together from more than 10 countries, bringing our unique strengths to the table.

Raising the bar

Solving hard problems requires relentless innovation. We constantly challenge ourselves to higher standards because our work is going to have incredible impact.

Working with grit

We strive for 10x excellence in our work. We look for those who are better than us, as we accomplish amazing amounts of important work.

Pushing ourselves

We are curious as a team. We challenge each other and ask questions to get to the root of problems. And we learn and relearn with an open mind in these ever evolving times.

We question, learn and relearn

We’re helping to build a wallet we’d love to use ourselves. We’re focused on solving difficult problems. We aren’t afraid to push the boundaries and challenge the status quo.
